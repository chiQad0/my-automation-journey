The Christian Medical and Dental Association of Nigeria - Students' Arm (CMDA Nigeria - Students) 
is a dynamic Christian body consisting primarily of Medical and Dental Students with a vision aimed 
at caring for the whole man: spirit, soul and body with over 3500 members across over 40 chapters 
nationwide. It is an arm of Christian Medical and Dental Association of Nigeria (CMDA Nigeria).

Our activities include carrying out medical missions, health campaigns, rural outreaches and missions support amongst others.
Opportunites abound for individuals and corporate bodies to partner with us in carrying out our outreaches.
